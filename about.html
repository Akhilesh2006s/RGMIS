The RGIMS Story

" Founded in 2015 by the visionary Dr M. Santhiramudu, RGMIS was born out of a deep commitment to redefine education and inspire young minds to reach their fullest potential. Dr. Santhiramudu's vision, built on over 30 years of success in education, spanning schools, medical colleges, and engineering institutions, led to the establishment of RGMIS, bringing world-class education to the heart of the Rayalaseema region."


" In the decade since its inception, RGMIS has become a symbol of excellence. With a holistic approach to education, we have created an environment where academic rigour meets the joy of discovery, and every child is empowered to shine. Our unwavering commitment to fostering creativity, leadership, and resilience has transformed countless students into confident, compassionate global citizens."


" At RGMIS, we offer the renowned CBSE program, which lays a strong academic foundation for our students and paves their way to success. Our students consistently achieve outstanding results, often ranking among the top in the district. They are exceptionally well-prepared for admission to leading universities in India and abroad, a testament to the quality of education at RGMIS."


" Today, RGMIS is more than a school; it is a community united by the pursuit of knowledge, character, and excellence. As we celebrate a decade of inspiring education, we remain steadfast in our mission to shape a brighter, more inclusive future for every child who walks through our doors, instilling hope and optimism in our students and their families. "

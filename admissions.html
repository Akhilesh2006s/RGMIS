Admissions

At RGIMS, we strive to make the admission process transparent and straightforward for parents and students. Our aim is to identify and nurture talent while ensuring a seamless journey into our academic community.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Grades Offered: Admissions are open for Grades I to XI for the academic year 2025-26.
  • Academic Requirements: Students must have successfully passed their previous grade with a minimum of 60% marks.
  • Curriculum: Applicants must have studied under the CBSE curriculum or an equivalent system.
  • Cut-off Date: Admissions will close on 30 June of every academic year. Applications received after this date will not be considered.

Steps to Apply

Obtain the Application Form

Collect the application form from the school office during working hours.

Submission of Application Form

  • Fill out the application form accurately and completely.
  • Submit the form along with the required documents, including the previous grade's report card, transfer certificate, and identification proof.

Screening Test

  • Students applying for admission will be required to appear for a screening test in English and Mathematics.
  • The test evaluates the applicant's foundational understanding and readiness for the applied grade.

Evaluation and Admission Offer

  • Students must meet the required score in the screening test to qualify for admission.
  • Upon qualifying, parents will be notified, and the admission process will be completed after payment of the fees.

Important Notes

  • Admissions are conducted offline only. All applicants must visit the school for submission of documents and the screening test.
  • Seats are limited and allocated on a first-come, first-served basis to eligible candidates.
